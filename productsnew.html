Hearing Aid Batteries

Hearing aids of all technology (from analogue to high-end digital hearing aids) use batteries. To simplify the process of making batteries and hearing aids to fit, manufacturers have come up with a standard for all hearing aids. This sizes fit all manufacturers.

Hearing aid batteries can be divided into 4 major sizes and grouped by colours

  • Blue Colour 675 - mostly for Behind-The-Ear Hearing aids
  • Orange Colour 13
  • Brown Colour 312
  • Yellow Colour 10 - only for Completely-In-The-Canal Hearing aids.

So if you do forget the size of your hearing aid batteries, remember the colour. There are many manufacturers of hearing aid batteries but they all follow the standard for hearing aid battery sizes.

Assistive Listening Devices

Sometimes people who have hearing difficulties do not need hearing aids, then assisstive listening devices are introduced to help the person cope better. These include the FM system, Amplified phones, Infrared systems, TV listeners, stobe alarm, vibrating clocks, and more. Joyful Sound provides these devices as the need arises.

We focus on meeting the hearing needs of our clients and sometimes they are not looking for a hearing aid. These devices enhance the qualify of life of our clients.
