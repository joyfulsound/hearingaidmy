MP3 player and your ears
In line with the World Health Organization (WHO)'s theme for this year's Ear Day - Protect your hearing, Jensen Hearing is providing FREE assessment for those who use MP3 players. This assessment is done via the AXIOM Real Ear Measurement Device which allows us to see the level of sounds in the ear canal. The user is to being her/his MP3 player along for the assessment with the volume set at user preferred volume.

The assessment will allow us to know if the user preferred volume is within safety limits.
